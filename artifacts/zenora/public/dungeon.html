GAME RULES

🎮 CONTROLS

  • MOVE FORWARD:W or UP Arrow
  • MOVE BACKWARD:S or DOWN Arrow
  • TURN LEFT:A or LEFT Arrow
  • TURN RIGHT:D or RIGHT Arrow
  • ATTACK:SPACEBAR
  • PAUSE:ENTER

⚔️ COMBAT SYSTEM

  • Combat is turn-based: you act, then enemies act
  • Face an enemy and press SPACE to attack
  • Enemies will chase you when they spot you
  • Each attack deals 10 damage to enemies
  • Enemies deal 5 damage when they retaliate
  • You cannot walk through enemies - you'll bump into them

🎯 OBJECTIVES

  • Eliminate all monsters in the dungeon
  • Survive with your health points (HP)
  • Explore every corner of the maze
  • Use the environment to your advantage

💡 TIPS

  • Enemies can only see you within 8 tiles
  • Walls block enemy line of sight
  • Turn corners to break enemy pursuit
  • Attack enemies before they reach you
